My Projects

Beaver Runner is a 2d jump and run game refering to the "Biber Bonus Wochen 2024", a marketing campain by OBI. The game can be played directly from your browser here. For more information check out this repository which includes all the source code for the project and a more detailed explanation on how the project came to life.

Side.Games is a Projects and the "company" behind the games listed below that I started with a colleague of mine in 2020. Over the past years we published 3 games of different genres from a Platformer to a Shoot 'em up space shooter.

Choros is a 2d action space shooter game. Your goal is to stay alive as long as possibler while fighting off lots of enemies and bosses.

In S.A.C. you are a Secret agent who works for the company S.A.C.! Your mission is to prevent a new company from building a deadly weapon. In order to do so you have to steal documents from their offices and take out some of their employees.

Cat Adventure is a Run and Gun Plattformer Game in which you have to catch the mouse at the end of each level to get to the next one. The game features over 30 levels three different worlds and a bossfight at the end.
